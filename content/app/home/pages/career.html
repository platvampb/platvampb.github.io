Job responsibility

This person is joining our team as a technical partner. Their day-to-day work includes:

  • Working with Co-founder, Technology & Product to setup the full stack of our software;
  • Working as a senior Software Engineer to implement our SOA-based web apps;
  • Working with Co-founder, Technology & Product to build a development team;
  • Acting as technical lead of the development team;
  • Participating in the agile development process.

Qualifications

As a senior software engineer in the team, the skill and knowledge set we are looking for:

  • 3+ years web development experience;
  • Intimate and working understanding of modern web applications from database to browser;
  • Strong SOA and MVC experience;
  • Experience with Cloud application development;
  • Experience with https and network security;
  • Keen on development best practice;
  • Proficient in Java, preferably in the Spring and Hibernate framework;
  • Strong experience with front-end development frameworks, preferably Angular JS or React;
  • Experience with Node.js a plus.

The ideal candidate is a:

  • Person with strong passion in startup and team building experience;
  • Person that aligns with our company values;
  • Person that enjoys a fun environment, but values efficient & good flow;
  • Person that values code review and is positive to provide and accept feedback from code review process;
  • Person that is a knowledge giver, and also is eager to learn;
  • Creative and curious problem solver that loves challenging development projects.

The bonus points include:

  • Experience with Continuous Integration;
  • An open source contributor;
  • Spark experience;
  • Independently developed a production-grade web application of any size.

We offer

As an early-stage partner, you will:

  • Have the opportunity to participate in the process of building a team from scratch;
  • Have the opportunities to use the latest technologies to develop;
  • have a significant share of our company that is diving into a multi-billion dollars market, and we are the first in the industry to tap into this niche;
  • have the opportunity to develop into a role that incorporates greater responsibilities in the team;
  • Learn all the courses we learn in creating the company including business side, as long as you are interest in(knowledge sharing is one of our company values!);
  • experience the startup fun & excitement!
