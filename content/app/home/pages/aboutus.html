Mission Statement

This is where dreams are born and come true. This is where new life-style can be found. Get away from your repetitive work and boring day-to-day conversations; get into worlds you once imagined, but never realized. Our travel platform offers different ways to tingle your travel inspirations, which we help you move forward to bring your virtual inspirations to reality. With automatically generated smart itineraries centered around places you picked, a platform to recruit and collaborate with travel buddies from your social network, you will be kept on track as you move closer to your dream vacation with each item crossed off the to-do list we put together for you. Before you know it, you are already on your way.

As a company, we aim to build a sustainable organization that is integrated with digital technology with a mandate of creating value for all of our shareholders. As a travel community, we aim to build long term relationships with our users(our most valuable assets), by building online communities that offer much more than just travel research.

We believe that no part in a vacation experience should be frustrated, and our travel eco-system provides a new life-style to make people's life a lot happier, easier, and then they can recharge themselves and focus on their own works, which will in turn make other people's life happier, easier.

Company Values

We recognize the following as our company value, and strive to provide such a work environment where employees can benefit from the company's success, which will in turn further company's success.

TRAILBLAZER
When we face difficulty, when we face uncertainty, when we face wilderness that nobody ever walked on, we are the trailblazer.

HUMOR
Humor is the best weapon to combat boring work and make office life happier. We believe a happy office life is extremely important in order to bring quality and creativity to our work.

CUSTOMER OBSESSION
We always want to understand customer's pain point, and solve customer problems by delivering our service.

TEAMWORK
Hey, no one is gonna be successful on his own. Even Batman has his Robin.

KNOWLEDGE SHARING
Knowledge sharing between team members is the best way for everybody to learn and grow. We definitely want you to grow with us.

HONESTY
To be honest, you made a mistake in your code at line 145. To be honest, you did very well in your last meeting. We are not putting fingers, but honesty welcomes positive criticism and improves productivity.
