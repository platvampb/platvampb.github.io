Company values

We recognize the following as our company value, and strive to provide such work environment that employee can benefit from company's success, which in return also promote company's success.

Humor
Humor is the best weapon to combat boring work and make a happier office life. We believe a happy office life is extremely important to bring quality and creativity to our work.

Teamwork
Hey, no one is gonna be successful on his own. Even bat man has his Robin.

Knowledge sharing
Knowledge sharing between everyone is the best way for everybody to learn and grow. We definitely want you to grow with us.

Honesty
To be honest, you made a mistake in your code at line 145. To be honest, you did very well in your last meeting. We are not putting fingers, but honesty promotes a positive critique and improves the productivity.

Trailblazer
When we face difficulty; when we face uncertainty; when we face wilderness that nobody ever walked on; we are the trailblazer.
